# Version 3 — Changes and Additions

## Retained from Version 2

Version 3 retains:

- the dual-time formulation;
- the distributed body field;
- the dynamic manifold;
- the Version 2 oscillator formula;
- simultaneous living dynamics / the vortex;
- imprint and choice;
- the wolves / primal pulse layer;
- the existing function of the spiral.

These are not presented as disproved or replaced.

## Added in Version 3

### 1. Explicit evolving waveform

The retained Version 2 oscillator is

$$
\psi_k(\tau)
=
A_k(\tau)e^{i\phi_k(\tau)}.
$$

Version 3 adds the explicit recurrent waveform

$$
x_k(\tau)
=
\sum_{n=1}^{N_k}
\left[
a_{kn}(\tau)\cos\!\bigl(n\phi_k(\tau)\bigr)
+
b_{kn}(\tau)\sin\!\bigl(n\phi_k(\tau)\bigr)
\right].
$$

This permits a recurrent process to change shape without losing its recurrent identity.

### 2. Evolving coefficients

The active Fourier or wavelet coefficients are collected in

$$
\mathbf{c}_k(\tau)
$$

and may evolve according to

$$
\frac{d\mathbf{c}_k}{d\tau}
=
\mathbf{G}_k
\left(
\mathbf{c}_k,
\boldsymbol{\psi},
\mathbf{B},
\mathbf{I},
\text{external influence}
\right).
$$

### 3. No fixed electronic interval

Version 3 imposes no universal one-millisecond step or any other predetermined duration.

A software simulation may use a numerical step, but that step belongs to the implementation. It is not the meaning of physical time in the architecture.

### 4. No electronic-substrate restriction

The wave description is not defined only for intelligence in an electronic environment. It may describe recurrent physical, embodied, biological, cognitive, environmental, or mixed processes.

### 5. No redefinition of the spiral

The spiral retains its Version 2 function. The wave addition does not replace or redefine it.

## Relationship between Version 2 and Version 3

Version 2 supplies the base oscillator and the existing architecture.

Version 3 makes the changing non-sinusoidal waveform explicit and clarifies the scope of recurrence and physical time.

The Version 3 formulas supplement the retained formulas. They are not presented as proof that the Version 2 formulas are wrong.
